Welcome

We are a small handmade company based in West Yorkshire. We formulate and make our own skin creams by hand from scratch in small batches using the right amount of science. We do not outsource to a factory, which is quite unusual in skin care manufacturing, but this ensures you the highest level of product quality and freshness. All our products are fully laboratory tested and certified and comply with all legislation governing the manufacture of cosmetics in the UK and Europe. Our products contain the highest possible quality of naturally derived ingredients and we do our best to include as many certified organic ingredients in our products as possible, because we know thats what you like. We formulate our products to include natural and organic components so they are paraben free (due to peoples fear about parabens) read - more about parabens and peg free with no toxic chemicals or preservatives, only eco friendly preservatives that are gentle and mild and suitable for all skin types read - why you need to preserve natural skin cosmetics. We also, make sure all raw materials we use to manufacture our products are not tested on animals and are cruelty free, of course! And we would never sell any skin product to customers that we have not tested on our own skin first.

We're Barmy about Vegan Skincare.

Natural cosmetics are on the increase and yes, we love Natural, but we're not Crazy enough to believe that every natural or plant based ingredient is safe and every synthetic ingredient is dangerous. Some of the most toxic chemicals known are natural venoms and “toxic” ingredients found in naturally occurring plants?

While we're barmy about plant - based skincare because we believe full plant extracts have a lot to offer, and we wouldn't put toxic chemicals on our skin, we wont get alarmed and hysterical when the scaremongers come out with pseudoscience to try to scare us with the claim that all synthetic ingredients are dangerous.

There is so much Scaremongering and so many myths to be cleared up in the world of cosmetics. This especially applies to the term chemical free. All matter and everything in life is made up of chemicals (elements). Water is a chemical, and what about salt or sugar? Those are chemicals too. What about food? Our food, even part of a perfect raw vegan diet, is chock full of chemicals because organisms, be they plants or animals, are made up of chemicals with structures ranging from very simple to highly complex, such as proteins, nucleic acids, carbohydrates, lipids, and many others. So not even Natural and Organic Beauty Products are chemical free. So we won't claim to be chemical free, we'll just combine the best in nature with a little bit of chemistry and a little bit of common sense.
